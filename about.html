    About Us

    LESBest is a lifestyle retailer dedicated to inspiring customers through a unique combination of product, creativity and cultural understanding. it was founded in 2022 by Tafon Hubert Nwana in Douala. We are currenly base at Bepanda-Ambiance Douala. We offer experient reatail enviroments and a well curated mix of on-trend women's and men's clothes, and also good and quality shoes for both male and female and other accessories.
